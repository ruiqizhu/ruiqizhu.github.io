In the summer of 2016, I interned at Plug and Play Tech Center, one of the largest Venture Capital firms in Silicon Valley. I worked as a cross border associate to bring U.S. portfolio startups to the China market, developed business strategy for the entry, connected startups with local Chinese corporations, organized a brain-tech sharktank event, and spearheaded the Plug and Play Open House event. Furthermore, I also helped organize the event that brought Silicon Valley Mayors to China to build partnerships with local Chinese government and incubators.
